INSTRUCTIONS

Description:

Bubble From a Hubble is an exciting and thrilling game that lets you tests your ability to think on your feet and strategize in a timed environment. Are you up for this enriching challenge? Play Bubble from a Hubble now and put your skills to the challenge!!


How to Play:

  • Click on PLAY button to start the game.
  • You will see a screen filled with bubbles having numbers from 0 to 9.
  • The target number to be hit is shown in header, left to the timer of 60 seconds.
  • You need to hit as many bubbles as possible in under 60 seconds.
  • After 60 seconds, you will be able to see your score.

Scoring Criteria:

  • For every correct target hit of a bubble, you will recive 10 points.
  • For every wrong target hit of a bubble, your score will be decreased by 10 points.
